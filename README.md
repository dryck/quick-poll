# 📊 Quick Poll

Get instant feedback from your students - no devices required for students!

## 🌐 Live Demo
https://dryck.github.io/quick-poll

## ✨ Features

- ⚡ **Instant Setup** - Create a poll in 10 seconds
- 📱 **No Student Devices Needed** - Display on screen, students raise hands
- 📊 **Live Results** - Watch votes come in real-time
- 🔗 **Shareable** - Students can vote on their devices too (optional)
- 💾 **Auto-Save** - Polls persist between sessions
- 🎨 **Multiple Formats** - Yes/No, A/B/C/D, traffic lights, and custom

## 📖 How to Use

### For Teachers

#### 1. Create Your Poll
- Go to "Create Poll" tab
- Type your question
- Choose preset or enter custom options
- Click "▶️ Start Poll"

#### 2. Display for Students
- Switch to "Vote" tab
- Project on your classroom screen
- Students see large, clear buttons

#### 3. Collect Votes
**Option A: Show of Hands**
- Read each option aloud
- Students raise hands
- Teacher clicks buttons to record votes

**Option B: Self-Voting**
- Students come up and click
- Or share the link for device voting

#### 4. View Results
- Go to "Results" tab
- See live bar charts
- Percentages update instantly

### For Students (Device Voting)

If sharing the poll link:
1. Open the link on any device
2. See the question and options
3. Click your answer
4. View results update in real-time

## 💡 Classroom Tips

**For Quick Checks:**
- "Show me on your fingers: 1-2-3-4"
- Teacher records votes on screen
- Instant visual of class understanding

**For Anonymous Feedback:**
- Students vote on devices
- No embarrassment about "wrong" answers
- Honest opinions on sensitive topics

**For Engagement:**
- Let students suggest poll questions
- Vote on class decisions
- Gamify with "predict the results"

**For Differentiation:**
- Confidence polls before independent work
- Identify who needs help
- Group students by responses

## 🔒 Privacy

- No student names required
- Votes are anonymous by default
- All data stays in your browser
- No account or login needed

## 🛠️ Technical Details

- **Built with:** HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript
- **Storage:** localStorage for persistence
- **Sharing:** URL-based with unique codes
- **No backend** - works completely offline
- **File size:** ~17KB
